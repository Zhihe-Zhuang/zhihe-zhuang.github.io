Education

I completed my B.Eng. studies in Automation at Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China, in June 2019. In September 2019, I continued my Ph.D. studies in Control Science and Engineering (first two years for M.Eng. studies) at Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China. In May 2025, I successfully defended my Ph.D. Thesis in Control Science and Engineering (first two years for M.Eng. studies) at Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China, titled "Iterative learning control and optimization of repetitive systems with varying trial lengths". My major courses include Automatic Control Theory, Process Control System, Motion Control System, PLC, Principle and Interface Technology of Micro Computer, System Identification, Linear System Theory and Design, etc.

During my visit to Oomen's group at Eindhoven University of Technology, I also joined master courses given by Prof. Tom Oomen, including Control Engineering (4CM00), Learning Control (4SC070), and Advanced Motion Control (4CM60).

I'm pursuing my Ph.D. degree at Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China. I expect to graduate in June of 2025.
